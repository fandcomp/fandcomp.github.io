Rundown

June 8, 2025 · Bintaro Xchange

Romantic & Fun Date at Bintaro Xchange

12:30 - Pick Up Alma

Arrive at Permata Oriana to pick up Alma. Make sure your appearance is perfect!

12:30 - 12:50 (Estimated) - Travel to Bintaro Xchange

Enjoy the journey with light conversation and play your favorite playlist. Estimated arrival at Bintaro Xchange Mall around 12:50 - 13:00.

13:00 - 14:15 - Romantic Lunch

Arrival & Parking: Find a comfortable parking spot.
Choose Restaurant: Bintaro Xchange has many culinary options. You can choose according to preference:
- Casual Dining: For a relaxed atmosphere (e.g., Japanese, Western, or modern Indonesian restaurant).
- Slightly Fancy: If you want a more special atmosphere.
Enjoy lunch while exchanging stories and jokes.

14:15 - 14:30 - Movie Preparation

After lunch, take a leisurely walk to the cinema (XXI). Buy tickets for the movie you've selected before (it's good to check the schedule and book online the day before if possible, especially since it's Sunday). Also buy popcorn and favorite drinks.

14:30 - 17:00 (Estimated, depending on movie duration) - Movie Time!

Enter the theater and enjoy your chosen movie. Quality time while watching together!

17:00 - 18:00 - Fun Time & Evening Walk!

Funworld/Game Arena: After the movie, it's time for fun! Invite Alma to play at Funworld or similar game arena available there. You can play basketball, car racing, or other exciting games.
Window Shopping & Strolling: After playing to your heart's content, continue with a leisurely walk around the mall. Look at shops, maybe there are cute items that catch your eye or just enjoy the mall atmosphere.

18:00 - 19:00 - Light Dining & Quality Talk

Find a Snacking/Drinking Spot: Choose a cozy cafe, ice cream/dessert stall, or trendy drink stand. This is the perfect time for deeper conversation, discussing the movie you just watched, or simply enjoying togetherness before the date ends.
Enjoy light meals while relaxing.

19:00 - Time to Go Home

Take Alma back to Permata Oriana. Thank you for the wonderful day and express hope to meet again.
